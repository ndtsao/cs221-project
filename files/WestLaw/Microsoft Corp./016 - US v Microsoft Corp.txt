ORDER
PER CURIAM.
*1 Upon consideration of the petition for writ of mandamus and motion for stay, the opposition thereto, and the reply, it is
 
ORDERED, on the court’s own motion, that No. 97-5343 and No. 98-5012 be consolidated for purposes of oral argument on April 21, 1998. It is
 
FURTHER ORDERED that consideration of the petition for writ of mandamus be deferred pending oral argument. It is
 
FURTHER ORDERED that the motion for stay of the December 11, 1997 order of reference be granted, provided that the instant order shall not serve to stay any discovery supervised by the district court, or any other proceedings in the district court. It is
 
FURTHER ORDERED that the parties be permitted to file supplemental briefs elaborating on any arguments in No. 98-5012. The briefs are to be hand-delivered and hand-filed simultaneously on Tuesday, April 7, 1998, and are not to exceed 3,750 words.
