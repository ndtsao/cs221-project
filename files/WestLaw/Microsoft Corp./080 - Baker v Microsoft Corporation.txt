ORDER
*1 Appellee Nintendo of America’s motion to dismiss this appeal for lack of jurisdiction (Docket Entry No. 8) is granted. See 28 U.S.C. § 1295(a); Breed v. Hughes Aircraft Co., 253 F.3d 1173, 1177–78 (9th Cir. 2001) (Federal Circuit has exclusive jurisdiction over patent appeals).
 
We decline to entertain appellant’s request for a determination regarding whether the February 1, 2017 notice of appeal was timely from a final decision of the district court.
 
All other pending motions are denied as moot.
 
DISMISSED.
