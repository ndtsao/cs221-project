ORDER
Appellant’s petition for rehearing by panel is granted. Further proceedings in this appeal will be held in abeyance pending the Missouri Supreme Court’s resolution of Watson v. Wells Fargo Home Mortgage, Inc. et al., SC–93769, and Conway et al. v. Citimortgage, Inc. et al., SC–93951.
