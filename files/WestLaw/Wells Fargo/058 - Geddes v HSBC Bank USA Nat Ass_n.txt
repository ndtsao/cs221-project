MEMORANDUM**
Richard and Delania Geddes brought a nine-count complaint in the Superior Court of Maricopa County, Arizona, seeking to stop the non-judicial foreclosure of their house after they defaulted on their loan payments. The case was removed to federal court. The district court granted the defendants’ motion to dismiss under Rule 12(b)(6) of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure. Geddes v. HSBC Bank USA, CV–12–0667–PHX–FJM, 2012 WL 1977277 (D.Ariz. June 1, 2012). The Geddes appeal. *758 Jurisdiction lies under 28 U.S.C. § 1291. We review de novo, Zadrozny v. Bank of New York Mellon, 720 F.3d 1163, 1167 (9th Cir.2013), and affirm for the reasons stated by the district court.
 
AFFIRMED.
