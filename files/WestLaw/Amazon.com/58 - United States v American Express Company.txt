ORDER
Appellee, United States of America, filed a petition for panel rehearing, or, in the alternative, for rehearing en banc. The panel that determined the appeal has considered the request for panel rehearing, and the active members of the Court have considered the request for rehearing en banc.
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the petition is denied.
