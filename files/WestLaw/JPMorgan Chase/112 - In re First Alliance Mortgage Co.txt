*882 ORDER*
**1 For the reasons given by the district court in its “Order Sustaining Objection to Proof of Claim and Disallowing Claim,” dated July 30, 2003, we affirm the district court’s disallowance of Mark Chisick’s unsecured non-priority claim against the bankruptcy estate of First Alliance Mortgage Co. and its affiliated entities.
 
AFFIRMED.
