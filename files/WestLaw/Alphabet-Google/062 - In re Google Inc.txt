ORDER
*1 Google et al. (Google) submits a letter of counsel in-forming the court of the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Texas’s order granting transfer of venue. Google further requests that its petition for a writ of mandamus be dismissed as moot.
 
Upon consideration thereof,
 
It Is Ordered That:
 
Google’s petition is dismissed as moot.
